Cryptocurrency is a form of payment that can be exchanged online for goods and services. Now we have understood about Cryptocurrency, let come to our main topic, Now everyone will be thinking that what is bitcoin?

The answer is Bitcoin is a digital currency which operates free of any central control or the oversight of banks or governments. Instead it relies on peer-to-peer software and cryptography a public ledger records all bitcoin transactions and copies are held on servers around the world. Bitcoin was the first cryptocurrency, later Ethereum, Litecoin, Dodgecoin etc were introduced. Bitcoin was introduced in 2008 and the founder of Bitcoin is also Satoshi Nakamoto.
